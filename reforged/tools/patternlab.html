Depth cues

Ground shadows
The fix for overlapping rings — shadows land at different distances on the floor even when the bullets overlap in view. Watch a shadow slide under you = that ring is at your plane.
Time-to-impact flare
Bullets warm from magenta toward white-hot in their last ~0.3s. The nearer of two overlapping rings flares first = it hits first.

Show parry reach volume
Reflectable bullets read cyan and flare bright when they enter the roll\u2019s reach. Parry then \u2014 Shift or the PARRY button. Reflected shots fly back into the boss.

Ring readability

Alternate ring colours
Successive rings cycle red \u2192 magenta \u2192 amber, so overlapping rings read as separate bands instead of one mesh. The biggest fix for tunnels. Works on the diamond bullets (via instanceColor).
Bloom (additive glow)
Your current look. Turn it off for dense rings \u2014 additive sums to white where bullets pile up (the blowout in your shot). Solid bullets keep their edges so you can count them.
