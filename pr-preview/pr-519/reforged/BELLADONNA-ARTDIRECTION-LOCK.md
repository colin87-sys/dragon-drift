## 1. THE ONE-SENTENCE READ + POSTURE LOCK

**A lethal wasp-DRAGON in horizontal chase flight — a draconic chitin skull-mask, a short
armored neck, and one pair of tucked raptorial claws on a gloss oil-slick violet-black dart;
four gossamer veined blade-wings in a shallow X, a pinched wasp-waist, a three-window venom
gaster glowing UV-orchid, and a needle stinger trailing dead-center at the lens.**

**The ruling in one line: the SKELETON is a dragon; the ANATOMY KIT is wasp.** She is a
dragon that evolved wasp anatomy — never a wasp scaled up. Dragon owns the head, neck,
limbs, and mass hierarchy; wasp owns the four wings, the waist, the gaster, the stinger,
the antennae, and the chitin surface language. Roughly 60% dragon chassis / 40% insect kit,
and the dragon half sits FORWARD (head/neck/shoulders — the parts that decide "what animal
is this"), the insect half sits AFT (waist/gaster/stinger — the parts that decide "which
dragon is this").

**FLIGHT POSTURE LOCK (horizontal — the aerial pair wins this axis):**
- **Body axis HORIZONTAL**: the nose-to-gaster line within ±8° of the flight path in cruise.
  The reared/upright S-pose in the banking images is REJECTED as a rest pose.
- **Head carriage**: level, gaze forward, carried slightly BELOW the thorax crown — the
  armored thorax is the highest dorsal point (thorax proud, per sheet §2).
- **The banking pair's S-curve is not discarded — it is ROTATED into the side profile** as
  the line of action: brow → thorax-crown rise → waist DIP (the concave break) → gaster
  swell → stinger under-curl that presents the needle up-toward the chase lens
  (`stingerPresent 0.4`). ≥1 inflection guaranteed; the drama of the hero card survives in
  the dorsal spline, not in pitch attitude.
- **Gaster/stinger trail**: the gaster hangs low-center BEHIND the rider line (sheet §10.2);
  the stinger is the tapering exclamation point dead-center in the rear fill.
- **Banks only**: the S-flourish is sanctioned as a TRANSIENT — the stinger swings INTO the
  turn (rudder with intent), both wing pairs sweep aft toward the ≤0.58 fold. This is where
  the banking images' energy lives in-game.

## 4. FEASIBILITY GUARDRAILS (the engineer inherits these as laws)

1. **Four wings: KEEP TRUE FOUR — the doublet-fused-as-one option is REJECTED.** The
   nullable `parts.auxWingPivots` rig hook (sheet §11/§R3) is scoped, additive, and
   roster-byte-identical when absent; the 0.35 phase-offset hum is the roster's only
   four-wing shimmer and the rear four-spoke X *requires* independent rakes (fused pairs
   collapse to two fat blades = a bat). SETTLED backs this: descoping the hook descopes
   the dragon. Hind pair stays 0.62× linear, 0.28 body-lengths aft, one plate-thickness
   below, 12° flatter; fore/hind planform overlap ≤20% (the rear-separation guard).
2. **Sacs = opaque emissive FILL MESH behind a SINGLE-layer translucent wall (0.72), 3 sacs
   maximum** — never a translucent glowing volume, never stacked walls, never a hex-grid of
   small panels. Apex transparent inventory holds at ≤8 drawables (4 membranes + 3 sac
   walls + trail).
3. **Wasp-waist floor**: apex `waistPinch` 0.34 with a hard floor of 0.22× thorax radius;
   petiole ≤0.10 body length; mid-tier paint (never the darkest hex); one dorsal strake
   continuous through the pinch. The concavity is the identity — the severed-body read is
   the veto.
4. **Stinger thickness**: a 4–6-sided loft, base ≈0.5× gaster seg-4 radius, sheet-sanctioned
   taper to 0.10×; the 2 lancet barbs (×0.8 pair) break the mid-length; the venom channel
   is an inset seam, not a painted stripe. No 2-tri blades anywhere on the tail.
5. **Dragon-not-bug is structural**: ONE pair of lofted, clawed, knee-up-tucked forelimbs
   (failure modes 2 + 7 killed by construction); a draconic muzzle-wedge skull-mask with
   brow + mandible blades; **horn-gauge antennae** — base thickness ≥0.12× head width,
   swept back 30–40°, canted ±12°, taper to ≤0.15× base (taper law), `antennaeLen`
   ladder 0.4→1.0 unchanged. Thin-feeler antennae are a veto.
6. **Anti-mosquito locks (from §R/§F, restated as the build bar)**: forewing chord ∈
   **[0.24, 0.30]× wing length** (hind inherits via 0.62 scale); thorax silhouette width
   ≥0.5× forewing chord; rear black-fill mass split **core (thorax+gaster+stinger+limbs)
   25–40% / four blades 50–65%**, asserted per form. Under 25% core = mosquito; over 40% =
   grub with fins. Both are vetoes.

## 5. PALETTE + VENOM-GLOW LOCK (sheet §9 reaffirmed, cruise/Surge doctrine explicit)

- **Chitin lacquer ladder (body, value DOWN)**: `0x241430 → 0x1f1129 → 0x1a0e23 → 0x150b1d`
  (f0→f3); belly/venter dark plum `0x2f1a38`; ≥0.05 luminance spread between tiers (CP4
  endpoint law), judged on the brightest biome + the dark shop card.
- **Oil-slick sheen**: diffuse grazing-row tints only — violet `0x4a2a68` / teal glint
  `0x1e4a4e`, ≤10% coverage, roughness 0.35 band on the dorsal streak (0.7 elsewhere).
  NEVER the full-body iridescence shader (Astral's).
- **UV-orchid accent `0xd936ff` (292°)**: fill-line hi `0xe86aff`, deep brew `0x8a1eb0`,
  drip bead `0xef8aff`. Zero warm hues, zero green, zero gold. ≤9% surface at apex.
- **Where glow lives (closed list)**: (a) the 3 gaster sac FILLS — always-on, luminosity
  earned by rising VOLUME (`sacFill` 0.05→0.30→0.60→1.00), never intensity cranking;
  (b) the eyes; (c) the drip bead at the stinger tip from f2 (2.2s deterministic
  swell-and-cull cycle); (d) the stinger channel seam from f3; (e) the pterostigma
  wing-spot ×4 from f3 (the only wing emissive, ≥8px). Nothing else emits, ever.
- **BREWING read**: at every rung the fill SURFACE line is the brightest pixel on the
  gaster (`0xe86aff` vertex-lit) falling to `0x8a1eb0` at depth — a real liquid from two
  tris of gradient. The fill line IS the diegetic power meter; f0's dreg-glimmer (0.05)
  hints the apex.
- **Cruise vs Surge**: cruise = the still glows at its fill level + eyes, wings dark.
  Surge ("Venom Overdrive") = fill emissive ×`surgeGlowMultiplier` + 2 bead motes streamed;
  **wings stay dark** (`feverWing 0x000000` kill-switch) and the FULL fever palette is
  overridden (`feverEye`, `feverWash [0.07,0.02,0.10]`, `surgeHi 0xef8aff`); eyes stay out
  of the surge arrays.
