# Nimbus — The Cloud Whelp
key: nimbus · rarity: R (free starter) · cost: 0 · maxRarity: SSR · forms: 3
fantasy: the first friend you fly with — a round, eager cloud-pup who grows into a
swift little storm-runner without ever losing the big-eyed charm.

## Shape recipe (written FIRST — proportion before decoration)
primary/secondary shape: circle 70 / triangle 30 (Gate-1 language: rounded apex curves)
motif: the CIRCLE, at 3 scales — round head dome, round-scalloped wing trailing edge,
round cloud-puff tail tip
silhouette signature: a big round head on a compact teardrop body with short broad
wings and a puff-tipped tail (nothing else in the roster leads with a circle)
hero feature (A3): **cloud-puff tail tip** — a soft vapor tuft that carries the accent

## Proportion targets (§6, designcheck-bound)
head:body ≥ 0.28 (big-head cute mode, A2) · wingspan 1.9× body (D2) ·
chest:hip ≥ 1.5 (D1) · tail taper ≥ 4:1 into the puff (A1) ·
spine bend 30–40°, one soft S (D3) · wing chord ≥ 0.25 half-span, 4 fingers,
concave trailing edge (D2)

## Palette (C1 60/30/10 · C2 ΔL ≥ 15)

## Evolution story (3 forms, R → SSR)
F0 Hatchling: nearly spherical whelp, stubby round wings, bare puff nub — pure circle
F1 Kindled: body lengthens, wings fan out (still round-scalloped), puff glows faintly
F2 Radiant (SSR apex): sleek storm-runner — the circle motif holds (head dome, rounded
wing arcs) but the wings broaden and a soft coral spine-glow + full cloud-puff arrive

## Playability envelope
Compact silhouette, no long trailing parts that read as obstacle language; readability
gates (readability.mjs) confirm at build time. Puff is soft-glow, never bloom-heavy.
